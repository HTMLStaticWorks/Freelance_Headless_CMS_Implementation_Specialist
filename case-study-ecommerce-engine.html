E-commerce Integration

Architecting a Composable Shopify Engine.

Building a lightning-fast product catalog using Sanity.io and Shopify Hydrogen for a luxury fashion brand.

The Challenge.

The brand needed to combine rich, editorial storytelling with a robust commerce backend. Traditional Shopify themes were too restrictive for their high-end aesthetic, and they needed a way to manage complex product relationships and marketing content in one place.

Implementation Strategy

We implemented a "Best-of-Breed" stack. Sanity.io was used as the Content Authority for all marketing and product metadata, while Shopify handled the core commerce logic (checkout, inventory). We synced data using Shopify webhooks and a custom middleware layer.
