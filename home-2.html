Technical FAQ.

Do you use GraphQL or REST?

Both. I implement REST for standard webhook triggers and simple fetch requests, while utilizing GraphQL for complex, relational data queries to prevent over-fetching.

How do you handle database migrations?

I use version-controlled schema definitions and automated CI/CD pipelines to ensure database migrations sync perfectly with your Next.js frontend deployments.

Can you integrate with custom Auth providers?

Absolutely. I frequently integrate Auth0, Clerk, and NextAuth directly into the headless CMS middleware to ensure secure, role-based access control (RBAC).
